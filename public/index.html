How It Works

The Recovery Journey

From the moment you lose an item to the moment it's back in your hands, we ensure full transparency.

1. Report Online

Submit a secure report with photo evidence and pinpoint the exact GPS location of the loss on our map.

2. Police Match

Police officers cross-reference reported items with found inventory using smart matching algorithms.

3. Instant Alert

Receive an automatic email notification when your item is found, with instructions for collection.
